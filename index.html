Welcome!

The 10th Mid-Atlantic Student Colloquium on Speech, Language and Learning (MASC-SLL 2023) is a one day event bringing together students, postdocs, faculty and researchers from universities and industry in the Mid-Atlantic area. The colloquium is an opportunity for students and postdocs to present preliminary or completed work and to network with other researchers working in related fields.

This year MASC-SLL will be held at George Mason University (GMU), Arlington campus, Virginia, on Saturday, April 22nd, 2023. There is no registration charge, and refreshments and lunch will be provided.
